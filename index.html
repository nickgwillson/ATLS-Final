How It Works

 

1.

Show Up

Each of our programs begin by honoring a social agreement that sets the expectations for how we’ll show up for ourselves + others.

2.

Learn


We facilitate group learning around a framework or resource that deepens our understanding of diversity, equity, and inclusion.

3.

Take Action

Leave feeling empowered with new ideas to explore and an action plan for moving forward.

You’ll Never Be The Same…

We believe in bringing people together to challenge the status quo.

That’s why we’re building a new culture of inclusive leaders who use listening, communication, and facilitation skills to radically shift people’s perspectives and create pockets of change in their own communities.

When you join one of our programs, you can stop feeling stuck, helpless, and like nothing’s ever going to change — and instead get the tools you need to take action.
